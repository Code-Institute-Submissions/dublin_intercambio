About

Hello and welcome to Dublin Intercambio. Dublin Intercambio was set up by 2 friends who met 3 years ago while doing a language exchange. Juan moved to Dublin and was looking to practise his English, while Niamh was learning Spanish and was looking for opportunites to pracitise what she had been learning in class with native speakers.

They both gained so much from their exchanges that they decided to set up Dublin Intercambio and provide the opportunty here in Dublin for Spanish and English learning to get together and learn from each other.

We know from personal experience that taking the leap from learning a language in classroom or online to actually putting yourself out there and have real life, spontaneous converstations with native speakers can be daunting. Our aim is to create a relaxed, friendly and welcoming environment, a space where you feel comfortable to take that leap and see how your language skills soar!

Why Language Exchanges?

Classes and text books are great for learning grammar points, conjugations, etc. but to really take your language skills to the next level you have to start talking and ideally talking with native speakers of your target language. Often in class there isn't much time for talking, the only native speaker is the teacher (if you are lucky!), and you are normally constrained to specific topics. The aim of learning a new language it to communicate with people, language exchanges provide the opportunity to take everything you have learned in the classroom and put it into practise.

Top 5 Reason to come to a Dublin Intercambio event!

  • The best way to learn a language is by actually speaking it
  • It is complete FREE! Well you may have to buy a drink but apart from that it is free.
  • One of our friendly team will be there to meet you and make sure you have a group to chat with
  • Great opportunity to meet like minded people
  • Having real life converstaions highlights your strengths and weakness. So you now what to brush up on before the next event!

It takes time to learn a language, so it's important to have fun and enjoy the process, otherwise you can lose motivation. So what are you waiting for, come along to our next event and see for yourself how quickly your language skills improve!
