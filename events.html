Gas Work
Mondays @ 7pm

Every Monday night we meet at The Gasworks pub. We are a friendly, welcoming group and there are always some moderators there to introduce you to people, start converstations and make sure everyone has someone to chat to. No need to register just turn up, grab a drink and start chatting!

Skype
Wednesdays @ 7pm

Every Wednesday night we meet online. One of our lovely team will host the meeting and have some themes in mind to get the conversation flowing. Numbers are limited for this one so if you want to take part drop us a message via the contact page and we will send you the details to join. If it is full, we will add you to the waiting list.
